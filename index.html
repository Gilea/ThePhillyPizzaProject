Neapolitan Philadelphia, PA

Sorellina

The dough at Sorellina is the real deal — airy, well-fermented, and consistent across every pizza we tried. But the kitchen struggled: two burned pizzas, a 40-minute wait, and a near-empty restaurant on a Saturday night. The Diavolo proved the potential is there.
