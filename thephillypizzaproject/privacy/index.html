Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

The Philly Pizza Project ("we," "us," "our") operates thephillypizzaproject.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us via our contact form. This includes your name and email address. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer this information to outside parties.

Cookies

We use a single local storage key to remember your dark mode preference. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that collect personal information. Google Fonts is loaded from Google's CDN, which may set its own cookies subject to Google's privacy policy.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is used solely to send you review notifications. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Use the contact form on our contact page.
